Vitor Hadad
Postdoctoral scholar
Stanford University
Graduate School of Business

I'm an economist with experience in a variety of data science and machine learning problems, focusing in particular on personalized recommendations and adaptive experimental design. My projects have ranged from the very practical (e.g., promoting modern contraceptives in developing countries) to the theoretical (e.g., statistical analysis of data collected via multi-armed bandits). I've led multi-disciplinary teams in collaboration with different institutions, including the New York City Department of Finance and the World Bank. I'm interested in deploying the appropriate statistical tools to solve real-life issues.
